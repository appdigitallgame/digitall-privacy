Frequently Asked Questions

How does the daily puzzle work?
Every day a new puzzle is available. You have 6 attempts to guess the correct IP address using 4 mathematical clues. The puzzle resets at midnight UTC.
What are the colored cells?
Green means the digit is correct and in the right position. Yellow means the digit exists in the number but in a different position. Red means the digit is not in the number.
How do hints work in training mode?
You get 3 hints per day. Each hint reveals the correct value for one block of the puzzle. Hints reset daily.
Can I play offline?
Training mode works offline. Daily puzzles require an internet connection to sync the puzzle and verify the date.
How do I change the theme?
Go to Settings (gear icon on the home screen) and toggle "Dark theme" to switch between dark and light appearance.
My data was lost after an update. What happened?
This is rare and may happen during major updates. Your daily streak and achievements are stored locally on your device. Please contact us if this happens.
